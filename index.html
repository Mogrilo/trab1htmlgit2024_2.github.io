BEE MOVIE SCRIPT

The Bee Movie follows Barry B. Benson, a honeybee who is disillusioned with the repetitive life of making honey. After venturing outside the hive and discovering the human world, Barry befriends a florist named Vanessa and learns that humans exploit bees for honey production. Outraged, he files a lawsuit against the human race to stop honey exploitation. Barry wins the case, but this disrupts the natural balance as flowers die without bees pollinating them. Realizing his mistake, Barry and Vanessa work together to restore the world's flowers and return the bees to their vital role.
